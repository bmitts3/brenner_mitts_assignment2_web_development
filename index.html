Lacrosse

Where did it originate?

Lacrosse orignated as a native american sport and was played as early as 1100 AD. It was played primarily in the Northeast of what is now Canada and the United States.

How to play?

Lacrosse is played with 10 people from each team. There is a goalie, 3 attackmen, 3 defense, and 3 midfielders. There can only be 6 field players on either side of midfield at any given time. The goal is to throw the ball into the opposing teams goal using your lacrosse stick.

Required Equipment

  • Helmet
  • Chest Protector
  • Elbow Pads
  • Gloves
  • Cleats
  • Lacrosse Stick
  • Uniform
